Best Places to Birdwatch in Utah County

  1. Utah Lake State Park
    • Waterfowl and Shorebirds
  2. Vivian Park (Provo Canyon)
    • Riparian Birds
  3. The Alpine Loop & Mt. Timpanogos
    • High-elevation Birds
  4. Bicentennial Park (Provo)
    • Songbirds and Raptors
  5. Sandy Beach & Airport Dike Road
    • Shorebirds
